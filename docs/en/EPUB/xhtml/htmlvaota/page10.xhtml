9. The Search Kit

Once you have identified that you need to conduct a search for chemical waste ensure that you have the following items at hand to undertake the search (if available).

  • Video and still camera with a tripod
  • Storage cards (SD)
  • External batteries and cables
  • Evidence bags
  • Exhibit item numbers (can be handwritten)
  • Safety equipment and clothing
  • Stationary (pens, notebook, markers)
  • Evidence Continuity Forms
  • Crime Scene Tape
  • Bolt cutters
  • Trolleys
  • Gamma radiation survey instrument
  • Organic vapour monitor
  • Combustible gas meter

You will use some or all these items when searching for and documenting chemical waste products. It is better to be prepared and have them readily available in a search kit.

You may also be required to seize large quantities of chemical waste products such as drums, or containers that will need transportation to another location. You will need to take this into account in your planning.
